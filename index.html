Arrears under Custodial Reassessment & Custodial Advocacy Reform Act

About the Reform

This act modernizes custodial policy and addresses arrears reassessment, family advocacy, and justice reform — complementing the SUPPORT and CARA legislation.

Core Objectives

  • ✔️ Equitable reassessment of arrears
  • ✔️ Enhanced support for family reunification
  • ✔️ Transparent advocacy and legal aid access
  • ✔️ Interlink with CARA & SUPPORT federal programs
